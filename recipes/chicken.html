Creamy Garlic Tuscan Chicken

Description

This Creamy Tuscan Garlic Chicken tastes like it is straight from a restaurant.
The first time I made it, I could not even get over how amazing the creamy sauce was!
It has such a delicious creamy garlic taste with spinach and sun dried tomatoes.
The chicken is so tender and juicy every bite just melts in your mouth!
Everyone who eats this chicken falls in love with it at first bite!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • tomatoes
  • light cream
  • white whine
  • spinach

Preperation

  1. turn on stove
  2. cook the chicken
  3. make the sauce
  4. add the spinach
  5. add the wine
  6. mix it all together
  7. enjoy
