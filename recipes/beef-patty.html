Jamaican Beef Patty Recipe

Description

These are delicious curry-flavored beef pastries that can be found in Jamaica and other Caribbean islands.
Try serving them with some Peas and Rice, or just have them as a snack.

Ingredients

  • beef
  • dough
  • curry
  • water
  • thyme

Preperation

  1. turn on oven
  2. prepare patty
  3. put patty together
  4. put in oven
  5. take out oven
  6. let it cool
  7. enjoy
